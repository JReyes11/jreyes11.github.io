Hi, I'm Jose

I've worked in the quality space for over 20 years as a Senior Quality Engineer, Software Development Engineer in Test (SDET) and Quality Assurance Manager. In the ever evolving world of Software Development, the complexity of applications and the speed of delivery continue to increase. From a quality perspective, this typically means there is more to test, and less time to test it.

So how do we keep up? At times the solution is adopting new tools and frameworks. In some cases, it's about finding ways to integrate testing more closely into the development process, whether that's through shift-left testing, continuous integration, or other DevOps practices.

This blog is where I share practical write-ups on tools, frameworks and process adjustments I've found useful over the years.
